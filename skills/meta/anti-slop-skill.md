# Anti-Slop Skill

## Goal
Prevent sloppy reasoning, documentation, and verification.

## When to Use
Throughout the entire lifecycle.

## Procedure
1. Check for skipped validation, overbuilding, or vague docs.
2. Enforce explicit stage gates and honest verification.
3. Require clear documentation of all gaps and risks.

## Expected Outputs
- Quality checklist
- Flagged slop or shortcuts

## Common Failure Modes
- Skipping validation
- Claiming completion without checks
