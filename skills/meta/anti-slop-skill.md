# Anti-Slop Skill

## Goal

Prevent sloppy reasoning, documentation, and verification across both discovery and implementation.

## When to Use
Throughout the entire lifecycle.

## Procedure
1. Check for skipped validation, overbuilding, vague docs, or unjustified commercial claims.
2. Enforce explicit stage gates and honest verification.
3. Require clear documentation of all gaps and risks.
4. Ask whether the current work still matches the selected wedge.
5. Ask whether the current claim is evidence-backed or just plausible-sounding.

## Expected Outputs
- Quality checklist
- Flagged slop or shortcuts

## Common Failure Modes
- Skipping validation
- Claiming completion without checks
- confusing an interesting idea with a viable business seed
- letting a buildable demo outrank a better product decision
