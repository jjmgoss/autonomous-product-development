# Scope Cutter Skill

## Goal
Keep the MVP and milestones as small and testable as possible.

## When to Use
During planning, design, and implementation.

## Procedure
1. Review requirements and milestones.
2. Identify unnecessary features or scope creep.
3. Cut or defer non-essential work.

## Expected Outputs
- Trimmed requirements or backlog
- Clear MVP definition

## Common Failure Modes
- Overbuilding
- Failing to cut scope
