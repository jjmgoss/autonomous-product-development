# Validation Skill

## Goal
Decide if the chosen idea deserves to be built.

## When to Use
After opportunity selection, before requirements and design.

## Procedure
1. Assess evidence for and against the idea.
2. Decide go/no-go based on:
   - Strength of evidence
   - Competing solutions
   - User motivation
   - Smallest prototype to falsify thesis
3. Document explicit go/no-go decision.

## Expected Outputs
- `docs/validation.md`
- Go/no-go decision

## Common Failure Modes
- Skipping validation
- Building without strong evidence
