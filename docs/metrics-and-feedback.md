# Metrics and Feedback

Use this file to think beyond "it runs on my machine."

## Include

- what signals would indicate users find value
- what early product metrics matter for the MVP
- what commercial signals matter, including willingness to pay or repeated use
- what user feedback channels are available
- what operator-burden signals matter, such as support load or manual cleanup
- what qualitative feedback would be enough to justify iteration

## Rule

Do not invent metrics just to sound serious.
Pick a small number of meaningful signals tied to product value, commercial plausibility, and operational burden.
