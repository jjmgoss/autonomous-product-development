# Product Brief

Run ID: 20260422-investigate-internal-recruiting-operations-r1
Selected seed: internal recruiting workflow operations

## Product Name Or Working Title

Hiring Loop Coordinator

## Target User

In-house recruiter or people ops lead at a small or mid-sized company where hiring managers and interviewers contribute asynchronously to the decision.

## Painful Workflow

After interviews happen, the recruiter still has to chase evaluation forms, remind busy interviewers, escalate blocked decisions, and assemble enough context for a hiring manager to make the next call before the candidate goes cold.

## Job To Be Done

Given a completed interview loop, help me collect the missing feedback, identify the blocker, and move the candidate to the next decision with less chasing and less silence.

## Core Product Wedge

A workflow layer that sits on top of the existing ATS and communication tools to:
- assign or confirm required interview feedback tasks
- remind and escalate when feedback or decisions are late
- summarize the candidate packet for decision makers
- show one clear owner and next deadline for every active candidate loop

## Success Criteria For The MVP

- Recruiters can see which candidate loops are blocked and by whom.
- Missing feedback requests and reminders are triggered automatically.
- The product produces a candidate decision packet that reduces manual chase and recap work.
- Teams can keep internal feedback within 24-48 hours and candidate updates within 2-5 business days for the pilot roles.

## Explicit MVP Scope

- Post-interview workflow only
- Existing ATS remains the source of truth for candidate and job records
- Feedback routing, reminders, escalation, and packet generation
- One or two communications channels such as email and Slack
- One small-team buyer segment rather than agency recruiting or enterprise campus programs

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Replacing the ATS
- Sourcing candidates
- Full interview scheduling platform
- Broad talent analytics suite
- HRIS, payroll, or onboarding workflows

## Monetization Angle

Team subscription priced for active internal recruiting teams that want faster, more disciplined hiring loops without replacing their ATS.

## Why Agent-Led Build And Operation Are Plausible

The core work is coordination and summarization. Agents can generate reminders, detect stalled loops, summarize interview notes, and propose next actions without field ops or deep human services.

## Top Risks And Adoption Barriers

- Customers may expect this inside the ATS they already pay for.
- Integration burden can expand the scope quickly.
- If the reminders and summaries are not trusted, teams will fall back to manual chase behavior.
